Volunteer Opportunities

We help people save time and stress by providing high quality volunteer programs that will help more people to give their time.

Hands On Park

Hands On Park is a unique and innovative 15 acres of lake front land in the City of Orlando. Our aim is for people to reconnect with nature, learn about the local environment and participate in Hands On activities that include STEAM related events.

Local Businesses

The goal of the CFSA is to provide hands-on experiences, during times that are convenient for the business and on a regular enough basis to make sustainable, positive impacts in Central Florida.

Corporate Volunteering & Team Building

Hands On Orlando is Central Florida's leader in managing corporate volunteer projects. The goal: helping the community by impacting the lives of people who need your help across Central Florida.
